Overview of Our Project

Our system leverages advanced AI and machine learning algorithms to analyze X-ray images for musculoskeletal abnormalities. With a focus on precision and speed, we aim to assist healthcare professionals in making accurate diagnoses effortlessly.

Key features include user-friendly analysis tools, detailed diagnostic results, and a confidence rating for each abnormality detected. Empowering medical teams with state-of-the-art technology, our project ensures better patient outcomes.
